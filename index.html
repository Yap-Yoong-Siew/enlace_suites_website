ABOUT US

Pantai Sentral Park is a 58-acre integrated development ,next to the 200-acre Bukit Kerinchi Forest Reserve. Located between Kuala Lumpur, Petaling Jaya, and Bangsar, it offers easy access to business hubs, entertainment, and daily conveniences. Enjoy the best of city living while staying close to nature.
